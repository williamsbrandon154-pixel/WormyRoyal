Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

What We Collect

WormyRoyal.io collects minimal data to operate the game:

  • Nickname: The display name you enter (not stored permanently)
  • IP Address: Used temporarily for connection management and rate limiting. Not logged or stored.
  • Game Data: Snake position, score, and actions during gameplay (exists only in memory during the session)

What We Do NOT Collect

  • No accounts or registration required
  • No email addresses
  • No cookies or tracking
  • No personal information
  • No analytics or third-party scripts
  • No advertising

Data Storage

All game data exists only in server memory during active sessions and is permanently erased when the game room closes. We do not store any data to disk or databases.

Third Parties

We use Google Fonts for typography. Google may collect standard web request data (IP address) when fonts are loaded. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Children

WormyRoyal.io does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game requires no account and collects no personal data.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page.
